Guest Management System for minstry of innovation and technology ethiopia

During my internship at the Ministry of Innovation and Technology, I developed a robust guest management system to streamline the process of managing guest visits and officer availability. The system's key features include: Receptionist Capabilities: Register detailed information about guests upon arrival. Capture real-time guest images or guest ID images. Send guest requests to officers and await responses. Officer Interaction: Receive guest requests and respond with either: Positive Response: Including the office location for the meeting. Negative Response: Providing reasons and potential follow-up details. Guest Experience: Guests are guided through the process based on officer responses. After completing their tasks, guests can provide ratings. Admin Panel: View guest ratings and system analytics. Manage user accounts for officers and receptionists (create/delete accounts). This system significantly improved the efficiency of handling guest visits and enhanced the overall experience for both guests and officers.

Online food ordering system for debremarkos university

I developed a comprehensive online food ordering system tailored for Debre Markos University. The system is designed to streamline the food ordering process and enhance user experience with the following features: User Capabilities: View, search, and sort available food items. Place orders and make payments seamlessly. Upload ratings to provide feedback on food items. Receive personalized recommendations based on the most ordered and highly rated items. Additional Functionalities: Cashiers: Can send successful order notifications to users and the kitchen to start preparation. Waiters: Manage completed food orders, send notifications to users, and delete completed orders from the system. Admin Panel: Provides full control over the system, including user management, order tracking, and overall system maintenance. This project was designed with scalability and efficiency in mind, ensuring a smooth and intuitive experience for all users, from students and staff to administrators.

Accountant website for a person to fasilitate his book-keeping

I successfully designed and developed a personal website for an accountant, incorporating an additional admin panel for efficient content management.
